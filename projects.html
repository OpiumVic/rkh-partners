Six programs.
Ready to break ground.

Upcoming · 2026

Mazao Mwangaza

Kiambu & Murang’a · Maize and dairy cooperatives

SMS-led farmer advisory paired with weekly USSD price snapshots. Designed to launch with twelve cooperatives and ~8,400 farmers, testing what advisory cadence actually moves a planting decision.

  • Advisory
  • Price data
  • USSD

Upcoming · 2026

Hub Sambaza

Machakos · Makueni · Kitui

A planned network of community hubs to serve as physical access points to digital services — advisory, financial inclusion onboarding, and a feedback loop into the platform’s product roadmap.

  • Community hubs
  • Digital literacy
  • Onboarding

Planned · 2026

Tabia Hewa

Six counties · ASAL belt

Localized climate information delivered alongside georeferenced farm profiles. A planned study with insurers on how decision-quality data shifts uptake of weather-indexed cover.

  • Climate
  • Insurance
  • Data

Planned · 2027

Wasichana Wajibu

Nyanza · Rift Valley

A program being designed with women-led producer groups to grow business literacy, leadership pathways and access to financial products — with peer cohorts as the unit of change.

  • Gender
  • Enterprise
  • Finance

Upcoming · 2026

Soko Smart

Nakuru · Uasin Gishu

Direct buyer linkages for horticulture cooperatives, with logistics partners and an aggregation desk designed to reduce the distance — informational and physical — between farm gate and final buyer.

  • Markets
  • Aggregation
  • Logistics

Pwani Roots

Coast region · Mixed cropping

A planned co-design phase with coastal cooperatives to map knowledge gaps in cassava, cashew and small-livestock value chains — setting the brief for the next program cohort.
