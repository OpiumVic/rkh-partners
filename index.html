01 — Who we are

A Nairobi-based social enterprise
turning rural knowledge into action.

Rural Knowledge Hub Partners exists to close the distance between what rural Kenya already knows and what it can act on. We work with smallholder farmers, cooperatives, institutions and partners to bring advisory, climate, markets, finance and data into one coordinated ecosystem — soil to system.

i. Place Nairobi, Kenya · serving 12+ counties
ii. Posture Inside existing systems, not parallel ones
iii. Promise Knowledge that moves a decision
Read our full story
i. Purpose

To ignite rural transformation by connecting people, data and opportunities through trusted, rural-driven knowledge and information ecosystems.

ii. Mission

To connect rural farming communities and enterprise with timely, practical and demand-driven knowledge & data — supporting collaboration, innovation, informed decision-making and inclusive development.

iii. Vision

To become Africa’s leading hub for rural knowledge, data and partnerships that inform decisions and drive inclusive, sustainable development.
