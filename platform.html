Platform · Vol. 01

One blended platform.
Many rural realities.

Digital where useful. Human where it matters. Four components working as one signal — from a farmer’s pocket to an institution’s dashboard.

Mobile · SMS · USSD · Field & cooperative network · Institutional dashboards
01 The pitch

Rural knowledge that moves when a decision needs to be made.

Rural Knowledge Hub Partners runs an integrated platform that brings advisory, climate, markets, finance, cooperative coordination and data onto one stack — designed to meet a farmer where they are and a bank where it plans.

We don’t replace what works on the ground. We layer digital and knowledge tools inside cooperatives, producer groups, extension networks and partner programs so the signal arrives in time to change a planting, a price, a loan or a policy.

The result: one ecosystem, two outcomes. Rural communities get timely, localised information and service linkages. Institutions get a clearer view of rural markets — and a delivery network they can design products and programs around.

“The information is there. The connections are not. We build the connections.”

RKH Partners · Platform thesis
02 How the signal moves

From the field, back to the field.

  1. →

    A need is named

    A cooperative, farmer group or partner flags a practical knowledge gap that’s blocking decisions.

  2. →

    A response is designed

    We co-create localised content, advisory flows, tools or data products with the people who’ll use them.

  3. →

    Channels are activated

    Mobile, SMS, USSD, field agents, cooperative meetings, dashboards — whichever channels fit the user.

  4. →

    Service linkages connect

    Farmers and enterprises are linked to buyers, finance, inputs, insurance and technical support.

  5. ↺

    Feedback returns

    Adoption, satisfaction and impact data flow back — refining the next iteration of the model.

01 / Digital
03 Digital channels

Mobile, SMS, USSD & dashboards.

Mobile app, short-message service, USSD, institutional dashboards and digital farmer profiling — built for low-bandwidth, multilingual rural use.

  • A. Mobile app with offline-first content and lightweight assets
  • B. SMS & USSD flows that reach feature phones across rural Kenya
  • C. Dashboards for cooperatives, partners and field teams
  • D. Digital farmer profiling & georeferencing for evidence-based design
02 / Assisted
04 Assisted channels

Call centres, field agents, community hubs.

Cooperative meetings, peer learning groups and on-the-ground support for users who need a human hand alongside the signal — because trust travels through people first.

  • A. Field agents trained in advisory and digital literacy
  • B. Cooperative-level peer learning & demonstration plots
  • C. Voice support in local languages for time-sensitive decisions
  • D. Community hubs that double as access points and feedback loops
03 / Linkages
05 Service linkages

Buyers · suppliers · banks · insurers.

Connections to buyers, input suppliers, financial institutions, insurers, agribusinesses, government programs and development partners — the platform isn’t an island; it’s a switchboard.

  • Markets
  • Inputs
  • Credit
  • Insurance
  • Grants
  • Extension
  • Logistics

Every connection is two-way: farmers gain access; institutions gain reach.
